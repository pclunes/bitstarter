What is this?

Give&Take is a non-profit project that aims to make donations easy. Everyone has things they don't use anymore: clothings, old toys, etc. A good way to get rid of them and be a good person is to donate them to someone who needs them. But how do you find where to donate these things? Often people donate clothings in places that need books or toys where they need old blankets.

Give&Take will make the idea of donating much easier and therefore, practiced by many more!

This is a very inspiring project that will help people help others :)

What is needed?

First, we need to build a website that can host all the magic. In other words, we need to build the portal and database that will allow people to join and find what they need. Secondly, we need to populate the site with data. To do that, we will contact charity organizations and find some marketing solutions to ensure we reach a lot of people!

The funding will be used to build the website and, if successful, we will proceed with the next stages of the project.

FAQ

What countries will be involved in the project?
Our goal is to make it a global plataform that can help people located anywhere in the world to make a donation.
Will the charity institutions be charged for a space in the website?
No. This is a non-profit project and the idea is to help charity organizations (and other individuals that may need a donation) to be known by those who have something to donate.
